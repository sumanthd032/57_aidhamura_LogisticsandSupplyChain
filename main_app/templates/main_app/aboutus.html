About Us

Who We Are

Welcome to Zeroplate we are a passionate team dedicated to tackling food waste and promoting sustainability in the food industry. We connect restaurants, hotels, and food service providers with charitable organizations, food banks, and community groups to redistribute surplus food. Our mission is to reduce food waste, support local communities, and create a more sustainable food system. By using advanced AI-powered recommendations, we help food providers make smarter decisions to minimize waste and maximize the positive impact of their surplus. With Zero Plate, we aim to create a world where food is never wasted, and those in need always have access to nourishing meals

Our Mission

At Zero Plate, our mission is to revolutionize the way the food industry handles surplus food. We strive to reduce food waste, promote sustainability, and provide access to nutritious meals for those in need. By connecting restaurants, hotels, and food establishments with charitable organizations, we create a seamless way to redistribute excess food. Through innovative AI-driven recommendations, we empower food providers to make smarter decisions that help cut down on waste, while simultaneously supporting local communities and enhancing the overall social impact. Our ultimate goal is to build a more sustainable future, where food waste is minimized, and hunger is combated

What We Do

  • Reduce foodwaste:At Zero Plate, we reduce food waste by connecting restaurants and hotels with charitable organizations to donate surplus food, ensuring that no food goes to waste while helping those in need..
  • Donate food:At Zero Plate, we facilitate the donation of surplus food from restaurants and hotels to charitable organizations, providing nourishment to those in need and reducing food waste.
